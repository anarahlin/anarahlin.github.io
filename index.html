I am an ornithologist and conservation biologist working at the Illinois Natural History Survey. My research focuses on the impact of land use and urbanization on migratory birds. I am using environmental DNA and metabarcoding methods to detect cryptic wetland birds, especially rails, to track their migrations and habitat use. I also research mechanisms causing red-headed woodpecker declines. email: rahlin1@illinois.edu mailing address: Illinois Natural History Survey 1101 W. Peabody Drive Urbana, Illinois, 61801
