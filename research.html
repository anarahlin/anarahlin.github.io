Research

My research focuses on the impacts of land use, urbanization, and climate change on migratory birds, cryptic wetland birds, and woodpeckers.
Browse below for project details.

Climate change and

aggression

Temperature influences behavior and aggression. We are interested in understanding how climate change influences competition and aggression between species, and how rising temperatures differentially influence endotherm behaviors.

Red-headed woodpecker

conservation

Red-headed Woodpecker populations are declining in the northern part of their range. We are investigating how urbanization, food availability, and competition affect woodpecker reproductive success.
